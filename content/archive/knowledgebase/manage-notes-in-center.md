<p>Use .Center to search for, create, edit, or delete your notes. To view <a href="https://humane.center/notes" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">your notes</a> in .Center:</p>
<ol>
<li>Log in to Humane.Center.</li>
<li>Navigate to the "Notes" tab at the top of the screen.</li>
</ol>
<h2 id="h_01HZ5D8YEDCA7BSJSS02G4K2F4">Create note</h2>
<ol>
<li>Select  in the top right. </li>
<li>Enter a title. If you plan on updating the list in the future, choose a title that's easy to remember, like "Grocery List" or "Weekend To-Do List."</li>
<li>Enter the note text. This is the text you want your Ai Pin to remember and recall in the future.  </li>
<li>Select Save. </li>
</ol>
<h2 id="h_01HZ5E06JSVWC9P9NV4MNVR3T3">Edit note</h2>
<ol>
<li>Select an existing note.</li>
<li>Select Edit and make any desired changes. </li>
<li>Select Save. </li>
</ol>
<h2 id="h_01HZ5E2B2TCZRZQ5SGBK2SGNVS">Delete note</h2>
<ol>
<li>Select an existing note.</li>
<li>Select   in the top right. </li>
<li>Select Delete. </li>
</ol>
<h2 id="h_01HZ5EGAGZYREK6525FP63T8GE">Search notes</h2>
<p>Use the search bar at the bottom of the Notes page to search for a specific note(s). </p>
