<p><strong>Summary</strong>: With the 1.2 release we will be introducing a world clock feature on Ai Pin. This functionality will allow you to know what time it is around the world, even when your device does not have a network connection. For example; “What time is in Paris, France” or “What time is it in London, England”. </p>
<p><strong>City Time Check:</strong> Curious about the time in another city? Simply ask, "what time is it in Bangkok, Thailand" even when you're offline. This allows you to stay connected with global time zones effortlessly, no matter where you are!</p>
