<h2 id="h_01JH155W66GP56VHJ7RGNS1MFJ">Summary</h2>
<p>Keep your payment method current to ensure uninterrupted service and avoid any billing hiccups. In this article, we’ll walk you through updating your payment details in just a few simple steps—so you can stay focused on what matters.</p>
<h3 id="h_01JH155W66ZC7614BC402PF9PJ">Before You Begin </h3>
<ul>
<li>Make sure you have your new payment information handy (e.g., credit card details).</li>
<li>Have your .Center login credentials ready.</li>
</ul>
<h3 id="h_01JH155W67HS26AMW35CNK3TJ1">Step-by-Step Instructions</h3>
<ol>
<li>
<p><strong>Log in to Humane.Center</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Open your preferred web browser and go to <a href="#" rel="noopener">Humane.Center</a>.</li>
<li>Enter your credentials to sign in.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Select Your Profile in the Bottom Right</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Locate and click on the profile icon in the lower-right corner of your screen.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Go to Settings, Then Plan &amp; Billing</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>In the dropdown menu or settings page, find and select <strong>Settings</strong>.</li>
<li>Navigate to <a href="https://humane.center/settings/account/plan"><strong>Plan &amp; Billing</strong></a> to manage your subscription information.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Click “Update” Under Billing Information</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Look for the <strong>Billing Information</strong> section.</li>
<li>Click <strong>Update</strong> to access the Humane Inc Billing portal.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Select “Add Payment Method”</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Once in the portal, choose <strong>Add Payment Method</strong>.</li>
<li>Input your new card details (e.g., card number, expiry date, CVV).</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Select “Add”</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Confirm your new payment method by clicking <strong>Add</strong>.</li>
<li>Your updated details will be saved automatically.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Click “Return to Humane Inc”</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>After updating, click <strong>Return to Humane Inc</strong> to go back to your .Center account.</li>
<li>You should now see your updated payment information.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
<hr>
<h2 id="h_01JH155W678HV1PYE9DEWPHNV7">Tips &amp; Troubleshooting </h2>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>Account Reactivation</strong>: If your account is inactive due to a payment issue, updating your payment method can help speed up the process of reactivating your subscription. However, you may still need to contact Humane Support to fully complete reactivation.</li>
<li>
<strong>Double-Check Your Details</strong>: Make sure your billing address and expiration date are correct to avoid any payment errors.</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<h2 id="h_01JH155W67DV5FTYBNVVKFW3DD">Conclusion</h2>
<p>That’s it! You’ve successfully updated your payment method, ensuring your subscription remains active and hassle-free. If you need help reactivating an account or run into any billing issues, reach out to <a href="#" rel="noopener">Humane Support</a> for assistance.</p>
