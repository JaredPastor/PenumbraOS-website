<p id="h_01HXSD3ZHHCWJK0GHCH4Z3RJZC">If you have trouble activating Ai Pin out of the box or unlocking Ai Pin to power <strong>OFF</strong>, we recommend resetting your Ai Pin. </p>
<p><iframe src="//player.vimeo.com/video/945863558?h=054f51c5b8" width="560" height="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen=""></iframe></p>
<div class="note warning"><span class="wysiwyg-font-size-large"><span class="wysiwyg-underline"><strong>Do not insert any tool into your Ai Pin</strong></span> – this can cause permanent damage to your Pin. Your tool should <strong>only</strong> be inserted into the Charge Pad.</span></div>
<h2 id="h_01HXSX2CER3XSHC9BC5CN1YA4Y">To reset Ai Pin</h2>
<h3 id="h_01J3DRC533PEHARMFV36KW3T6T">Using Ai Mic</h3>
<ol>
<li><span>Press and hold one finger on the touchpad. You will see the Trust Light flashing orange and hear a chime indicating Ai Mic is listening.</span></li>
<li><span>Say "power off."</span></li>
<li><span>Release your finger from the touchpad. The Trust Light will stop flashing and you will hear a chime indicating Ai Mic is no longer listening.</span></li>
<li>Attach your Ai Pin + Booster together and place them on a Charge Pad.</li>
<li>Wait a few minutes until you see the rainbow Trust Light, indicating your Ai Pin is powered <strong>ON</strong> again and ready for use. </li>
</ol>
<h3 id="h_01J3DRC8PGNQJ7X0V5WCCQMRMN">Using Charge Pad</h3>
<ol>
<li>Ensure that your Charge Pad is plugged into the wall outlet using the Cable and Adapter provided with your Ai Pin.</li>
<li>Place your Ai Pin and Battery Booster into the Charge Pad. Depending on Ai Pin's battery charge, a green or amber light will appear on the top of the charge pad to confirm the connection.</li>
<li>Insert a paperclip or SIM ejector tool into the small hole next to your Charge Pad's USB plug, and push in.<br><img src="/media/26695372741005.png" alt="CP1 reset pin.jpg" width="365" height="365">
</li>
<li>Continue pressing in until you see the light on your Charge Pad turn red. As soon as it turns red, you may let go.</li>
<li>Leave your Ai Pin alone on its Charge Pad. After 15-20 seconds, its Trust Light will display a cycle of rainbow colors.</li>
<li>At the end of this rainbow cycle, you may see three brief white lights, and you may hear Ai Pin make a faint sound.</li>
</ol>
