<p>You can located where your Ai Pin is located via the Pinpoint functionality within Humane.Center.</p>
<p>To enable Pinpoint:</p>
<ol>
<li>Log into your account on <a href="https://humane.center">Humane.Center</a> </li>
<li>Select your profile  in the bottom right corner</li>
<li>Select <strong>Settings</strong> </li>
<li>Select <strong>My Ai Pin </strong>  </li>
<li>Scroll down to "Locate My Ai Pin" and click <strong>Details</strong><img src="/media/30809953446669.png" alt="Screenshot 2024-10-04 at 2.18.06 PM.png" width="422" height="73">
</li>
<li>Toggle Pinpoint Location Tracking to <strong>ON</strong> and close the dialog by clicking  in the corner.</li>
<li>Now when you click on <strong>Details</strong>, a popup with map will appear with the last known location of your Ai Pin.</li>
</ol>
<p><img src="/media/30809163204621.png" alt="pinpoint.1.jpg" width="296" height="436"></p>
<h2 id="h_01J5Y1WMCFH9DJ4Q6QHM4MYGW0">Tips &amp; Solutions</h2>
<p>- Wi-Fi must be enabled in order for Pinpoint to function.</p>
<p>- Ai Pin does <strong>not</strong> need to be connected to a Wi-Fi Network, but the Wi-Fi must be <strong>ON</strong>.</p>
<p>- Ai Pin gets location from seeing what Wi-Fi networks it can "see" to geolocate.  </p>
<p> </p>
<h2 id="01JE72YN026B10QQ8RMH0XN7CT">FAQ</h2>
<p><strong>What if I do not use Wi-Fi to connect? </strong></p>
<p>That is fine, just make sure the Wi-Fi function is <strong>ON</strong> in the settings.<br><br></p>
<p><strong>What is the frequency of refresh for Pinpoint? </strong></p>
<p>There is no set update time as this reduces battery life. The location is updated when Ai Pin is awake and interacted with, and there are at least two Wi-Fi networks nearby.  </p>
