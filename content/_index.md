## About

Humane shut down all Ai Pin services on February 28, 2025. PenumbraOS is
a project by [agg23](https://github.com/agg23) to preserve
knowledge about the device and build new software for it.

This site is the central resource for everything Ai Pin -- from PenumbraOS
installation and development guides, to reverse engineering findings, to
archived official Humane content.
