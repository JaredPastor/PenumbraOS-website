<ol>
<li>Log in to Humane.Center.</li>
<li>Select your profile  in the bottom right.</li>
<li>Select settings  then under Account, choose Details  </li>
<li>Under Login Information, select "Update" next to your username or password. </li>
<li>When prompted, sign in with existing username and password. </li>
<li>Update with new username or password. </li>
<li>Select Update. </li>
</ol>
