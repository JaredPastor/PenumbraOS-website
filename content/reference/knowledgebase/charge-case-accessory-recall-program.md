<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left">    </p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left">Out of an abundance of caution, we are recalling the Charge Case Accessory in coordination with the Consumer Product Safety Commission due to an issue with certain battery cells supplied by a vendor for the Charge Case Accessory. The issue identified is isolated only to certain battery cells used in the Charge Case Accessory and is not related to the Charge Case Accessory hardware design. Importantly, Humane’s Ai Pin, its Battery Booster(s) and Charge Pad are not affected as the disqualified vendor does not supply batteries or any other components for those Humane products, and are safe for continued use.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left"> </p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left">Customer safety is our priority at Humane. Upon receiving a single report of a charging issue while using a third-party USB-C cable and third-party power source, we initiated an investigation and identified a quality issue with the battery cell supplied by a third-party vendor used in your Charge Case Accessory. </p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left"> </p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left">Our investigation determined that the battery supplier was no longer meeting our quality standards and that certain battery cells supplied by this vendor may pose a fire safety risk. As a result, we immediately disqualified this battery vendor while we work to identify a new vendor to avoid such issues and maintain our high quality standards.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left"> </p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left">Again, no other Humane product is affected by this recall. The issue identified is isolated only to certain battery cells used in the Charge Case Accessory and is not related to the Charge Case Accessory hardware design. Humane’s Ai Pin, its Battery Booster(s) and Charge Pad are not affected as the disqualified vendor does not supply batteries or any other components for those Humane products, and are safe for continued use.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left"> </p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><strong>Eligibility</strong></p>
<p>All customers of the Charge Case Accessory are eligible to participate in the recall. Customers will have the option to receive a replacement Charge Case Accessory or a refund. Upon confirmation of the customer’s preferred remedy, Humane will either send the customer a replacement Charge Case Accessory once they become available or issue a refund to the payment method linked to the customer’s Humane account. Simply click the link below to make your selection. </p>

<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><strong>Disposal</strong></p>
<p>Your Charge Case Accessory should be disposed of in accordance with any local and state ordinances, following the procedures established by your municipal recycling center for damaged/defective/recalled lithium batteries. Do not throw your Charge Case Accessory in the trash. Do not deposit your Charge Case Accessory in used battery recycling boxes found at various retail and home improvement stores.</p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left"> </p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><strong>Terms of Recall</strong></p>
<p>By participating in the recall, you are confirming that you qualify for the recall and that you will dispose of your Charge Case Accessory in accordance with any local and state ordinances. </p>

<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-center"><strong>FAQs</strong></p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left"> </p>
<p><strong>Why are you recalling the Charge Case Accessory?</strong></p>
<p>Out of an abundance of caution, we are recalling all Charge Case Accessory units. Upon receiving a single report of a charging issue while using a third-party USB-C cable and third-party power source, we initiated an investigation and identified a quality issue with the battery cell supplied by a third-party vendor used in your Charge Case Accessory. Our investigation determined that the battery supplier was no longer meeting our quality standards and that certain battery cells supplied by this vendor may pose a fire safety risk. As a result, we immediately disqualified this battery vendor while we work to identify a new vendor to avoid such issues and maintain our high quality standards. </p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left"> </p>
<p>While we know this may cause an inconvenience to you, we felt strongly this was the right thing to do. Customer safety is our priority at Humane. </p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left"> </p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left"> </p>
<p><strong>Are you recalling other products?</strong></p>
<p>No. No other Humane product is affected by this recall. The issue identified is isolated only to certain battery cells used in the Charge Case Accessory and is not related to the Charge Case Accessory hardware design. Humane’s Ai Pin, its Battery Booster(s) and Charge Pad are not affected as the disqualified vendor does not supply batteries or any other components for those Humane products, and are safe for continued use. </p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left"> </p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left"> </p>
<p><strong>How do I know if my Charge Case Accessory is recalled?</strong></p>
<p>All Charge Case Accessory units are subject to recall. </p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left"> </p>
<p class="wysiwyg-text-align-left"> </p>
<p><strong>I bought the Ai Pin Complete System. Do I need to return or dispose of everything?</strong></p>
<p>No. You only need to dispose of your Charge Case Accessory in accordance with local and state ordinances to receive a replacement or refund. </p>

<p><strong>My Charge Case Accessory seems fine. Do I need to participate in the recall?</strong></p>
<p>We are glad you have not had any issues with the Charge Case Accessory. However, we are asking all customers to immediately stop using the Charge Case Accessory and participate in the recall.</p>
