<p><strong>How to cancel your Humane Plan</strong></p>
<p>You can cancel your plan at any time.</p>
<ul>
<li>Upon cancellation, your plan will remain active until the end of your current billing cycle.</li>
<li>When your plan ends, Ai Pin will become inoperable.</li>
<li>You can continue to access the data in .Center after your plan ends.</li>
<li>You can reactivate your plan, but if you do not reactivate it within 60 days, your phone number will be rescinded. You would then need to purchase a new plan.</li>
</ul>
<p>To cancel your plan</p>
<ul>
<li>Log in to .Center.</li>
<li>Select your profile in the bottom right.</li>
<li>Select Settings then select <a href="https://humane.center/settings/account/subscription">Plan &amp; Billing</a>
</li>
<li>Under “Status,” you’ll see the option to cancel your subscription.</li>
</ul>
<p>For more information on your Humane Plan, visit this <a href="https://humane.com/humane-plan">article</a>.</p>
