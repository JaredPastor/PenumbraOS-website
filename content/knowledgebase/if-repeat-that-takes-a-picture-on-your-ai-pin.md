<h4 id="h_01JEYBCJ5X21QNEE2Z0C2H99YF"><strong>Summary</strong></h4>
<p>If your Ai Pin takes a photo instead of repeating its last response when you say "repeat that," this guide explains the issue and outlines alternative commands you can use to ask Ai Pin to repeat missed or misheard responses. It also provides additional steps to access your message history if needed.</p>
<h4 id="h_01JEYBCJ5XHKMTZCZRXANHNXTM"><strong>What’s Happening</strong></h4>
<p>The <strong>"repeat that"</strong> command is currently triggering the camera to take a picture instead of Ai Pin repeating the last response. This is a known issue that we’re actively working to address in an upcoming software update. Thank you for your patience as we resolve this.</p>
<h4 id="h_01JEYBCJ5XDC1AEV85W0ZFWSXD"><strong>Workaround</strong></h4>
<p>In the meantime, you can use the following steps to repeat messages from your Ai Pin:</p>
<ol>
<li>
<strong>Use "Say That Again"</strong>:
<ul>
<li>Say <strong>"say that again"</strong> instead of "repeat that."</li>
<li>Your Ai Pin will repeat the last message it delivered.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<strong>View Your Message History in .Center</strong>:
<ul>
<li>Visit Humane.Center.</li>
<li>Go to the <strong>Ai Mic</strong> section to view a full history of messages from your Ai Pin.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
<h4 id="h_01JEYBCJ5X5MEN2DNTNDBVMGDB"><strong>Additional Information</strong></h4>
<p>The issue is limited to the phrase <strong>"repeat that."</strong> Other commands, including <strong>"say that again,"</strong> are unaffected.</p>
