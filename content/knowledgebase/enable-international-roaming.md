<p>To use your Ai Pin outside of the U.S., purchase the International Roaming Pass and select how long you intend to be roaming abroad (1, 10, or 30 days). Once activated, you will have unlimited talk, text, and data for the duration of your pass. </p>
<p>For more details on international roaming and other available passes, see <a href="https://humane.com/humane-plan" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Humane Plan</a>.</p>
<p>To learn more about using your Pin abroad, see <a href="https://support.humane.com/hc/en-us/articles/24293846181389" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Traveling internationally with Ai Pin</a>.</p>
<h2 id="h_01J3E2K9NMPEJDPFWKQ9GSJ2G5">To enable international roaming</h2>
<ol>
<li><span>Log in to Humane.Center.</span></li>
<li><span>Select your profile  in the bottom right. </span></li>
<li><span>Select "Settings", then "Plan &amp; Billing."</span></li>
<li><span>Scroll to Premium add-ons and find "International roaming." Select "Add."</span></li>
<li><span>Select the desired package duration (1 day, 10 days, or 30 days) and the start date. You can choose today's date, or any date in the future. </span></li>
<li><span>Select "Add to subscription."</span></li>
<li>
<span>See the add-on under the Premium add-on section. If the start date is in the future, the add-on will appear as "Upcoming;" if purchased for today, will appear as "Active."</span><br><br><img src="/media/28658596440077.png" alt="Screenshot 2024-07-23 at 10.23.05 AM.png">
</li>
</ol>
