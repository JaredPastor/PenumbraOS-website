<h2 id="h_01J01G4ZQZYKRXKEWS7VE6WGE7">Summary</h2>
<p>With normal use, your Ai Pin might show signs of material transfer from objects that come in contact with your Pin, this can be things such as lint, dirt or other materials. This transfer of materials might resemble a scratch, but in most cases can be removed with some a few simple cleaning steps.</p>
<h2 id="01J22CAKMQG6FWEHWV9WZ73TMM">Steps to Take </h2>
<ol>
<li>Unplug all cables and turn <strong>OFF</strong> your Ai Pin.</li>
<li>Use a soft, slightly damp (water), lint-free cloth — for example, a lens cloth.</li>
<li>Apply light pressure and clean the affected area.</li>
<li>Avoid getting moisture in openings.</li>
<li>Don’t use cleaning products.</li>
</ol>
