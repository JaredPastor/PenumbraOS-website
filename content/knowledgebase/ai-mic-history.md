<p>Your Ai Pin is now smarter and more intuitive, letting you recall past activities. You can:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>Search Ai Mic history</strong> by date or specific details</li>
<li>
<strong>Search music history</strong> by date or description</li>
</ul>
<p>Our aim is to make your Ai Pin an extension of your memory—easily answering questions like "What song played last Friday?" or "What did I ask last week?"</p>
<p><strong>Current Search Capabilities </strong></p>
<ul>
<li> You can ask Ai Pin about past <strong>Ai Mic searches</strong> or <strong>music played</strong> from the last two weeks (up to <strong>1,000 events</strong>).</li>
<li>Only <strong>Ai Mic and Music events</strong> are searchable. These searches work in parallel with other memory queries for the most relevant results.</li>
<li>Complex queries like "How many Taylor Swift songs did I listen to?" and <strong>location-based searches</strong> are not supported yet.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>How to Turn ON Ai Mic Search History</strong></p>
<p>This feature is turned <strong>OFF</strong> by default but you can enable the feature <strong>ON</strong> your Humane.Center account page. To turn <strong>ON</strong> Ai Mic Search History:</p>
<ul>
<li>Log into your Humane.Center Account</li>
<li>Select your profile  in the bottom right corner</li>
<li>Select <strong>Settings</strong> </li>
<li>Select <strong>My Ai Pin </strong>  </li>
<li>Toggle "<strong>Ai Mic History Search</strong>" to the <strong>ON</strong> position</li>
</ul>
