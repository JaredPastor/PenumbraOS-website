<p>Ai Pin has the ability to link to your Google Calendar. You can learn about upcoming meetings, what your meeting schedule is for a given day, dial into meetings, and tell you the meeting participants. To activate the calendar recall function, follow the instructions below:</p>
<h3 id="h_01J47HKR6YY1V8YBARWC58MY0J">Setting Up Calendar Recall</h3>
<ol>
<li>
<strong>Log into Humane.Center</strong>:
<ul>
<li>Open your web browser and go to <a href="https://humane.center">Humane.Center</a>.</li>
<li>Log into your account using your credentials.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<strong>Access Services Menu</strong>:
<ul>
<li>Once logged in, click on the "Account Menu" icon in the lower right-hand corner of the screen.</li>
<li>Choose "Settings."</li>
<li>Click on the <em><strong>Services</strong></em> option from the left-side navigation bar.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
<strong>Link Google Account</strong>:
<ul>
<li>If you have already linked a Google account that you want to use, you will need to unlink and relink it.</li>
<li>Follow the prompts to link your Google account again.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ol>
<h4 id="h_01J47HKR6Y2WK28F9ZPWSJD37C"><strong>How to Use Calendar Recall on Ai Pin</strong></h4>
<p>Once Calendar Recall is set up, you can use it by asking the following questions:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<strong>Upcoming Meetings</strong>: “What’s my next meeting?”</li>
<li>
<strong>Meeting Participants</strong>: “Who’s in my next meeting?”</li>
<li>
<strong>Daily Schedule</strong>: “What’s my schedule for the rest of today?”</li>
<li>
<strong>Join a Meeting</strong>: "Join [insert meeting name]" or "Dial into [insert meeting name]."  Ai Pin will automatically enter the meeting passcode and dial into the meeting. </li>
</ul>
<p>These commands will help you manage your calendar and stay on top of your schedule. </p>
