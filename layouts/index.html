About

Humane shut down all Ai Pin services on February 28, 2025. PenumbraOS is a project by agg23 to preserve knowledge about the device and build new software for it.

The Knowledgebase section contains {{ len (where .Site.RegularPages "Section" "knowledgebase") }} of the original 97 Humane support articles, recovered from the Internet Archive. The remaining articles and most images were not captured before the site went offline.
